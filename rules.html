Rules

Breaking the rules will result in disqualification of you and your team. Please read carefully and make sure you understand them. If you have any questions, feel free to ask.

Project Guidelines
  • All of your code, designs, and custom assets must be created during the hackathon.
  • Utilization of third-party libraries and services is allowed as long as they are freely available to other participants and do not require any form of payment (ex: credit card).
  • You are free to use any technology to work on your project.
  • Your project must align with the theme of the hackathon.
  • You own your project, all the code you write, all the designs and assets you create, but by submitting, you allow us to showcase and promote it.
  • Web applications are encouraged, although all kinds of entries are welcome. If your project requires setup, you must provide a clear and detailed setup guide.
Submission Guidelines
  • All your work must be open-sourced on GitHub or an alternative, freely available online VCS platform.
  • You must include presentable screenshots of your project along with an optional (but recommended) demo video, showcasing its features. A detailed, straight forward setup guide or install script is also required if the project is not click-and-run.
  • Submit your project by the deadline; any submissions made after the deadline will NOT be evaluated. Only the latest submission will be considered.
Team and Participation Guidelines
  • Each team can have a maximum of three members. Individual participants are also welcome. If you decide to take part in a team, you all must enter the same names on the registration form.
  • Each participant can be a member of only one team.
  • Teams can be formed before or during the hackathon.
  • Changing teams is not allowed once registered.
  • Although not necessary, it is highly recommended to be a part of Lewis Menelaws' Discord Community as all of our communication will be conducted there first.

We reserve the right to update (refine) the rules at any time. If we introduce major changes, we will notify you on our Discord server.
